🇪🇹 Ethiopia Wave 1 · April 2026 · 4 publications
  • Available Now Ethiopia Flagship Report · Wave 1 · April 2026

    Understanding Ethiopia's Merchant Payment Landscape from the Ground Up

    The first nationally representative survey of Ethiopia's micro-enterprise merchant population — 2,724 merchants weighted to represent ~500,000 enterprises nationwide. Covers digital payment adoption, readiness conditions, channel architecture, provider concentration, the economics of merchant payments, and the credit access gap. 58.8% of merchants now accept digital payments, but rural adoption sits at 26%, 99% of digital flows land in personal accounts, and connectivity — not cost — is the primary barrier.

  • Available Now Ethiopia Commercial Intelligence Note · Wave 1 · April 2026

    Five Market Opportunities in Ethiopia's Merchant Payment Ecosystem

    Ethiopia's merchant payments market has crossed the majority threshold — but acceptance has not yet become value. More than 99% of digital merchant flows still land in personal accounts. This note identifies five commercial opportunities grounded in the baseline data: merchant credit for the missing middle, merchant account migration, rural expansion through low-tech distribution, supplier payment digitisation, and female merchant acquisition — the largest underserved segment. Written for banks, fintechs, telcos, and aggregators.

  • Available Now Ethiopia Launch Presentation · 21 April 2026

    MPO Ethiopia Wave 1 — Launch Event Deck

    The public launch presentation for Wave 1 findings, delivered on 21 April 2026. Also outlines the MPO's three-wave longitudinal research programme and planned dissemination activities.

    MPO Ethiopia Wave 1 — Full Findings Deck

    The comprehensive 128-slide research deck covering all chapters of the Wave 1 survey: merchant profiles and business context, digital payment readiness, adoption and behaviour, channel utilisation and onboarding, barriers to adoption, the value proposition of digital payments, value-adding services, credit access, and cash-based enterprises. Includes full methodology and data notes.

    Available Now Nigeria Flagship Report · Wave 1 · June 2026

    Understanding Nigeria's Merchant Payment Landscape from the Ground Up

    The first nationally representative survey of Nigeria's micro-enterprise merchant population — 3,285 merchants weighted to represent all street-facing merchants nationally. Produced in partnership with EFInA, the report covers digital payment adoption, readiness conditions, channel architecture and provider utilisation, barriers to adoption, the economics of merchant digital payments, and the credit and business financial services gap.
