  • Available Now Ethiopia Baseline Survey · April 2026

    Understanding Ethiopia's Merchant Payment Landscape from the Ground Up

    58.8% of Ethiopia's merchants now accept digital payments. But rural adoption sits at 26%, 99% of transactions flow through personal accounts, and connectivity — not cost — is the primary barrier. This report maps the full picture across readiness, adoption, provider concentration, and the structural factors shaping merchant behaviour at the last mile.
